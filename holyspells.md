# Holy Spells

Holy Spells are taken directly from
[Dolmenwood](https://www.dolmenwood.necroticgnome.com/rules/doku.php?id=holy_magic),
with a few edits and exceptions as follows:

## Rank 1 Spells

**Light** can blind any creature, not just Chaotic creatures; the saving throw
is Willpower 14.

**Mantle of Protection** works against all attacks, not just attacks by Chaotic
or summoned creatures.

**Rally** requires a Willpower 14 saving throw.

## Rank 2 Spells

**Hold Person** requires a Willpower 14 saving throw.

**Silence** requires an Arcana 14 saving throw.

## Rank 3 Spells

**Bless Weapon** adds 1 weapon damage die (instead of dealing an extra 1d6).

## Rank 4 Spells

**Circle of Protection** works against all attacks, not just attacks by Chaotic
or summoned creatures.

**Serpent Transformation**'s summoned Adder's poison requires a Constitution 11
saving throw.

## Rank 5 Spells

**Holy Fire** requires a Willpower 14 saving throw.

**Holy Quest** requires a Willpower 14 saving throw.
